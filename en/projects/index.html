Ongoing Project

Politiza-te

Co-coordinator · June 2024 – May 2025

Politiza-te is a civic awareness project that invites politicians and political figures to host Q&A sessions on topics such as the European Union, economics, political processes and more. The goal is to bring citizens — especially young people — closer to political and democratic life. Alongside this, we also invest in the digital format, publishing articles with simple explanations of economic and political concepts, and analyses of news and misinformation.

This project's initiatives and content are only available in Portuguese.
